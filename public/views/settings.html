Lava Wash

Use left and right arrow keys to move, space-bar to jump. Lava Wash is A 3-D Side scrolling game, avoid the enemy, find the key, and get to the door before the Lava rises up and gets you. background objects and backdrop built with a three.js using WebGl renderer(if compatible). Character models built with Blender for the character model creation. Only a few levels made so far, but more to come. Although, all updated browsers *Should* be compatible. Sign up to track how your doing against other people who have played!

Through the Glass

An immersive 3-D platforming game. Find your guide, avoid the lasers, remove the threats, and find the door to advance. New items appear often through the levels, more diverse enemies, and creative ways to destroy them. explore to figure out different ways to solve the puzzels and advance! built with three.js and WebGl. built for practice using 3-D framework through the browser using the GPU to render the graphics in order to produce better graphics through the browser.
